If you want the full version of one of our special libraries, you need to purchase a license. This license will be associated to your computer only.

**Step 1 - Getting your computer ID**

Download the following application (built in AnyLogic) to obtain your computer ID. Make sure you run this in the computer you are going to use AnyLogic in:

[Download the Computer ID application from Dropbox](https://www.dropbox.com/scl/fo/sm018dsyqek0ezcbqvh4k/AL3t7mAnUa7c4F9WGGBmLF8?rlkey=grapj1hbydvtviohxi51a6n4a&dl=0)

Be aware that an internet connection is necessary to use this software. When you run the executable file, you will see the following interface that will show you what your unique computer ID is, and you will be able to click the button to copy that ID (so you don't have to write it down manually)

![](/images/blog/getting-a-license-for-a-library/Monosnap-License-_-Simulation-AnyLogic-Professio.png)

**Step 2 - Paying and sending us your ID**

Now you have your unique ID, now it's time to pay. For now we are only accepting payments using PayPal, and you can easily create an account and place your credit card information to make payments.

We will expand the options and update the information on this page. In order to make the payment, you can use the following link:
[Pay via PayPal](https://www.paypal.com/paypalme/noorjax)

If you don't know the price of a library, please send us an email to [info@noorjax.com](mailto:info@noorjax.com) to get the information. Tell us what library you want, your country of residence and if you are a student or professional.

On the paypal page you will have to state a reason for the payment, please include there your email and the library you want to use. And make sure that email is the same as the one you are using to communicate with us. This is the email that will be associated to your license.

![](/images/blog/getting-a-license-for-a-library/paypal-1.png)

**Step 3 - Verifying your purchase**

Once you paid, we will create a token for you and we will send you an email with confirmation using the information you provided on the "what's this for" payment on paypal. If you don't get an email in 24 hours, contact us because it's highly likely that you didn't write the correct information and that's why we couldn't contact you.
Once you get our email confirming the purchase, you can then verify your token using the same application you used to get your id. You will see a list of the active licenses you have and get your token that you will use to activate your license for the library you want to use:

![](/images/blog/getting-a-license-for-a-library/Monosnap-Video-Preview-2024-09-09-20.45.48.png)

![](/images/blog/getting-a-license-for-a-library/Monosnap-Video-Preview-2024-09-09-20.46.11.png)

**Step 4 - Activate your library**

All libraries have a settings object that you need to use. The token you obtain will be used in the settings of that library as an input. You can read more in the library documentation. If you have issues, don't hesitate to send us an email.
